Our Mission

We believe essential document tools should be free, fast, and fully private. LocalPDF gives you 100+ PDF tools without asking for anything in return — no account, no subscription, no access to your files.

Why LocalPDF?

🔒 Your files never leave your device

Every tool runs using WebAssembly and JavaScript directly in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is sent anywhere. Ever.

🆓 Completely free, no catches

No trials. No watermarks. No file size limits behind a paywall. Every tool is free to use as many times as you need.

👤 No account required

Open the tool, use it, done. We don't ask for your email, name, or any personal information.

⚡ Fast, even offline

Because everything runs locally, performance depends only on your device — not a server queue. Works without an internet connection once loaded.

🌍 100+ tools, one place

Merge, split, compress, convert, edit, sign, encrypt, OCR and more — everything you need for everyday PDF work.

🌐 21 languages supported
